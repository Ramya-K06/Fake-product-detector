About Fake Product Detection

Fake Product Detection is a web-based system designed to identify counterfeit products using unique product identifiers such as QR codes, barcodes, or serial numbers. The system ensures that customers can verify the authenticity of any item before purchasing.

This project helps reduce fake products in the market and protects customers from low-quality or dangerous counterfeit items. It is useful for brands, shops, and consumers.

Main Features:

• Scan QR / Barcode
• Instant product verification
• Real/ Fake Result with details
• Secure database matching
• User-friendly interface
